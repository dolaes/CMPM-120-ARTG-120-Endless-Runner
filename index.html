You've Got Mail

You are Mail-E, an E-mail trying to navigate the internet to get to your destination.
However, the internet is plagued with viruses that you must avoid by switching between cables.
To help you along you're journey collect a VPN shield powerup to make you invulnerable to the viruses.

CONTROLS

Move Mail-E between the lanes using the ↑ and ↓ arrow keys to avoid the viruses.
